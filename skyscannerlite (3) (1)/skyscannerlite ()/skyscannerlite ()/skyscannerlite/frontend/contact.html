Frequently Asked Questions

How to book a flight?

Go to User Portal, enter source and destination in search, find your flight, note the Flight ID and Seat ID, then use the Book Ticket section.

How to cancel booking?

Go to User Portal, enter your Booking ID in the Cancel Booking section and click Cancel.

How to contact support?

Click on the WhatsApp button or use the Report Problem form above. We'll respond within minutes.

What are your support hours?

We provide 24/7 support via WhatsApp. Feel free to message us anytime!
